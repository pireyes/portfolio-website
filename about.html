About

My Story

I am a trans nonbinary UI designer. Before delving into the world of design, I worked in the education field for 6 years. In 2022, I decided to take a leap and pursue a career in design. I enrolled myself in a 9-month UI design program with CareerFoundry which kickstarted my journey as a designer. As a former educator, I am passionate about empowering others to reach their fullest potential. As a self-taught digital artist, I bring a unique set of creative abilities that greatly contribute to my role as a UI Designer.
