Soy Vegan

About The Project

Soy Vegan is a plant-based recipe web app that serves as an educational tool for cooks of all skill levels. Catering to different learning styles, Soy Vegan offers both video-based and written content for its users.


Problem Statement

Many recipe apps are not addressing the most important issue facing many young users today; accessibility. While there are many users who exist who can follow a written recipe effortlessly there are also those who exist that struggle with intaking written content.


Solution

The solution to this problem was to create content that is easy for any home cook to digest regardless of skill level. This means providing both video based content as well as written content to our users.


Research

The first step was to conduct user research with four users of digital recipes apps in order to identify pain points they may be facing with current recipe apps.

Interview Questions

  • How often do you cook at home?
  • What are some challenges you face when using recipe sites?
  • Are you a visual based or written learner?
  • What is your cooking skill level?
  • What is your biggest source of help when trying to discover plant-based recipes to cook?

Pain Points

  • Lengthy recipe instructions
  • Assumed skill level
  • Long inroductions
  • Ads and pop ups

Ideation

The next step was to create a detailed user flow of how our target audience would navigate the web app and perform tasks to reach their goal.


Wireframes

Next responsive wireframes were created to account for various breakpoints for the app.


Design

Onboarding Screens

The onboarding screens were designed to provide users with a quick and simple overview on the main features within the app. These particular set of screens introduce users to the “teach me” feature that teaches users simple chopping techniques.


Home Screen

The home screen was designed to be as simple and intuitive as possible. Users have the option to browse recipe directly from the home screen using the categories provided or search for specific recipes using the search bar.


Search Results Screen

The search results provide users with a wide variety of plant-based recipes to choose from.


Teach Me Feature

The teach me feature is introduced to users as a play button next to specific ingredients. A user has the option to interact with the feature which introduces users to proper chopping techniques.


Favorite Recipes Screen

With the “favorite” feature a user is able to favorite any recipe of their choice and access their favorites at any point. The user can access the favorites screen through the hamburger menu.
